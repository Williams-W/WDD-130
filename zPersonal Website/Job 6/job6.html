Vanity Checkout

Vanity

This sounds dumb, but I reccommend using a vaccum to clean the vanity drawers. In the guys apartments particularly there is tiny beard hairs that almost never come out with a cloth. They are gross and I gag when cleaning them. I have discoved that using a vaccuum is a fast way to remove the excess hair
The other thing that people miss all the time is the sinks. You need to scrub the entire sink and the counters. This means a deep scrub, not just a quick wipe down. All purpose cleaner works great on this task.
